

# Career Mentor — Visual Experience Plan

A bold, Gen-Z-coded career discovery app where students figure out who they want to be and get a personalized journey map to get there. Think Cash App's confidence × BeReal's honesty × a roadmap that actually feels like a quest.

## Visual Identity

**Aesthetic:** Bold & Gen-Z. High-contrast, expressive, sticker-meets-editorial.

- **Color palette:** A punchy core — deep ink black, off-white paper, electric lime as the hero accent, plus a hot coral and cobalt blue for category coding. One color does the heavy lifting per screen.
- **Typography:** Oversized display type for moments that matter ("you're a Builder."), tight sans for UI, occasional handwritten/marker accents for personality.
- **Texture & motion:** Sticker-style badges, tilted cards, subtle grain, soft drop shadows. Springy micro-animations on tap, confetti bursts on milestones, and a friendly bounce on the mascot.
- **Mascot:** A simple, expressive shape character (think a blob with eyes) that reacts to your choices — nods, gasps, celebrates. Lightweight, not cringe.

## Core Screens & Experience

### 1. Onboarding — "Let's find your thing"
Full-bleed color screens, one big question at a time. Name, age/grade, vibe check. Mascot introduces itself. Sets the tone: this isn't a test, it's a conversation.

### 2. Discovery — Mixed-format quiz
Three formats woven together so it never gets boring:
- **Swipe cards:** "Could you see yourself doing this?" — yes/no on activities and values, BeReal-style photo prompts.
- **Chat moments:** Mascot asks open questions in a texting interface; quick-reply chips keep it fast.
- **Day-in-the-life scenarios:** "It's 10am, you'd rather…" with three illustrated choices.

A slim progress bar at top with playful labels ("warming up" → "getting spicy" → "almost there").

### 3. Reveal — Your Career Match
Big dramatic moment. Confetti, mascot celebration, a bold typographic reveal of your top match (e.g., "you're a **Systems Builder**") plus 2–3 runner-up paths. Shareable card designed for Instagram stories.

### 4. The Roadmap — Winding journey path
The signature screen. A Candy Crush–style winding path scrolls vertically from "you are here" at the bottom to "dream job" at the top.
- **Stops** along the path = milestones (skills to learn, projects to build, courses, internships, certifications).
- **Locked vs. unlocked** stops with a subtle glow on what's next.
- Tapping a stop opens a **detail sheet** with what to do, why it matters, resources, and an estimated timeline.
- **Phase markers** ("Foundations," "Exploration," "Launch") break the path into chapters.
- Mascot walks the path with you, parked at your current stop.

### 5. Progress & Profile
Light gamification: a streak counter, milestones completed, a small collection of earned sticker-badges ("First Project," "Skill Unlocked"). No XP grind — just satisfying acknowledgment.

### 6. Mentor Chat
Always-accessible AI mentor in a clean chat UI — ask questions, get pep talks, re-route your roadmap if your goal changes.

## Interaction Principles

- **One decision per screen** during discovery — never overwhelming.
- **Big tap targets, thumb-friendly** — built mobile-first.
- **Celebrate small wins** with motion and the mascot, not noise.
- **Honest tone** — copy talks like a friend, not a guidance counselor.
- **Shareable by design** — match cards, milestone unlocks, and roadmap snapshots are all built to screenshot.
